FRC Team 2813 Prospect High School Gearheads

The Gearheads are a FIRST Robotics Competition level team that competes every year in FIRST regionals. We teach all interested students about every aspect of the team -- ranging from 3D design to photoshop to team publicity.

Giving high school students a critical hands-on experience in dozens of projects.

Every student interested in machines, hardware, photography, video-production, finances, business, public relations, photoshop, 3D design, programming has a job on the team!

Hands on Education

After participating in robotics, all students will have recieved hands on experience working with robotics projects during open workshops.

Building Leadership

There are more than a dozen board and lead positions that offer students a chance to lead a team and gain decision making skills, strengthen public speaking, and becoming more responsible.

Problem Solving

Students will be taught to adapt to numerous constraints and find new and innovative ways to solve commonplace problems, as well as finding elegant solutions to pressing complex isues the team faces through robot creation.
