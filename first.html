What is FIRST Robotics

FRC Team 2813

FIRST

FIRST is a program aimed for students from the elementary school level to the high school level. They run 3 divisions of FIRST: FIRST Lego League, FIRST Tech Challenge, and FIRST Robotics Competiton. We are a part of FIRST Robotics Competition, the high school level competition. The goal of FIRST is to recreate the recognition of excellence that can be found in physical activiteis and academics, but to reconize sudents for their excellence in technology an robotics.

Our FIRST Robotics Competiton (FRC) team operates as a club at Prospect High School & we are open to any all students and parents of PHS. We invite all students to come to any of our meetnisg s or wirkshops, regardless of prior knowlege or past experience in robotics in general our our club itself. Our mission is to povide a hands-on experience to all students at Prospect High School in the fields of STEM and engineering.
